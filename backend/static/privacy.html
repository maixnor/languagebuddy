2. Information in Accordance with Art. 13 and 14 GDPR

For our service to function, we require and process the following personal data:

  • Your master data: Last name, first name.
  • Contact data: Your phone number, which is essential for using the WhatsApp-based service.
  • Payment data: We do not store your credit card or bank details ourselves. Payments are processed exclusively via our partner, Stripe. Stripe processes data related to the payment method and in connection with payments.
  • Chat data: The content of your conversations with the Language Buddy chatbot via WhatsApp will be saved to provide and improve the service.

This data is necessary for our services. This includes providing the chatbot functionality, managing your user account, processing payments, and personalizing the learning experience.

We do not use profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you. Your chat data is used to improve the general service for all users, but not to make automated decisions about you specifically.

The legal bases for these data processing activities are:

  • The fulfillment of our contractual and pre-contractual obligations to you in accordance with Art.6(1)(b) GDPR (i.e., providing the Language Buddy service you signed up for).
  • Your explicit consent obtained from you at the beginning of our interaction ("ACCEPT") in accordance with Art.6(1)(a) GDPR.
  • Our statutory, contractual, or other legal obligations in accordance with Art.6(1)(c) GDPR (e.g., documentation obligations under tax and accounting law).
  • Our legitimate interests in accordance with Art.6(1)(f) GDPR (e.g., improving our customer service and the AI model, or protecting our own legal interests).

The duration of storage is determined by the duration of our business relationship, the consent you have given, and the statutory retention requirements and legal obligations that apply to us (e.g., financial data must be retained for 7 years under Austrian law). We retain chat data to ensure continuity of service and for service improvement, until you request its deletion.

Important Note on Data Transfers to the USA:

The European Court of Justice has ruled that the level of data protection in the USA is not equivalent to that within the EU. In particular, there is a risk that your data may be accessed by US authorities for control and monitoring purposes, possibly without any legal remedy.

By replying 'ACCEPT' and using our service, you explicitly agree to the transfer of your personal data (phone number, chat data, payment information) to these necessary service providers in the USA for the purpose of fulfilling our contract with you, in accordance with Art.49(1)(a) and Art.49(1)(b) GDPR. If you do not agree to these terms, we will unfortunately not be able to provide the service to you.

4. Your Rights as a Data Subject

You can request information about your personal data stored by us at any time, free of charge. As a data subject, you also have the following rights:

  • Right to Rectification: You can correct any inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be forgotten"): You can request the deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that we provide your data to you in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to our processing of your data. Since we process data for direct marketing (e.g., service updates), you can object to this at any time.

You may revoke your consent for data processing at any time. A revocation means that we will no longer process your data for the purposes stated from that point on. The legality of the processing carried out up to the time of revocation is not affected by the revocation.
